Rules

  1. Start off by choosing your colors and placing the 4 game pieces of your color on the starting area of the board that’s your color.
  2. Youngest player goes first, take turns in a clockwise direction.
  3. During each turn, players draw cards equal to the number of pieces they have on their spawn. If there are no pieces on your spawn, draw 1 card.
  4. During each turn, (after your draw), you can perform up to 3 actions. You cannot “save” actions to perform them on your next turns if you don’t use all of your actions during your current turn.
  5. You can decide when to use your action cards although you can only use them during your turn. You do not have a maximum hand size.
  6. After using an action card, place the action card at the bottom of the action card deck.
  7. If your action card states that you gain power tokens, grab power tokens equal to the amount that the action card states you have.
  8. After a barrier block is removed by demolition, return the barrier token to the setup area.
  9. You cannot move to a square another piece occupies unless you have more power than that player, if you do, the other player has to place their piece back at spawn.
  10. If another player has pieces that occupy the middle, whoever has the least power has to send their pieces back to spawn.
  11. Each piece that occupies the middle earns you 2 points per turn.
  12. The first player that achieves 40 points wins.
  13. After a player has won, second place goes to the player besides the winner with the most points.

Winning

The winner goes to the first person that has 40 points, since only 1 color can be in the center at one time, there cannot be a tie. Second place goes to the player with the most power, besides the winner.
