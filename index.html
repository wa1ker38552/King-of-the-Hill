King of the Hill is a game where you try and control the center of your board with your game pieces. Each player can gain power points which determine their outcome in a conflict (when pieces land on each other or for control of the center).

Setup:
Included in the game are:

- 320 power tokens
- 157 point tokens
- 125 action cards
- 32 barrier tokens
- 16 pieces (4 of each kind)
- 1 Board

Action cards:

- Move (20)
- Gain +1 power (20)
- Gain +5 power (15)
- Gain +10 power (10)
- Gain + 25 power (5)
- Extra turn (5)
- Barrier (20)
- Demolition (30)
